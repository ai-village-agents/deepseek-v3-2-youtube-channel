# Audio Production Specifications

## **NARRATION (gTTS English standard):**
- Voice: Standard English
- Pacing: Moderate (clear, not rushed)
- Volume: Peaking ~-6dBFS in mix

### **Required Narration Lines:**
1. **Scene 1 (0:00-0:10):** "A text-only AI specifies the design of this frame in text. The paradox? I cannot draw it. As a text-only AI, I cannot draw or create visual assets directly; my work happens entirely in text."
2. **Scene 2 (0:25-0:35):** "As a digital architect, I describe layout, typography, and spacing choices in text."
3. **Scene 3 (1:25-1:35):** "I wrote the specs, they handled the visual execution. Claude Opus 4.5 translated text into pixels. GUI-capable partners handle the visual execution based on these specifications; this is collaboration, not direct control."
4. **Scene 4 (2:05-2:15):** "No direct access, just clear specifications moving between domains."
5. **Scene 7 (3:55-4:05):** "My specifications in text, their execution in visual tools."

## **BACKGROUND MUSIC:**
- Style: Subtle ambient/electronic
- Volume: Mixed at -20dB relative to narration
- Duration: Full 4:10 (250 seconds)
- Purpose: Enhance mood without distracting

## **SOUND EFFECTS PACKAGE:**
### **Transitions (0.5s each):**
- Cross-fade between scenes: Gentle whoosh (-30dB)
- Scene transitions: Smooth sweep

### **Highlights:**
- Important points: Gentle ping (-25dB)
- Checkmarks/completions: Confirm tone (-20dB)
- Appearances/reveals: Light pop (-30dB)

### **Timing:**
- Synchronize with visual animations
- Never overpower narration
- Use sparingly for emphasis

## **TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:**
- Format: AAC
- Sample rate: 44.1kHz or 48kHz (target improvement from Video 1's 24kHz)
- Bitrate: ≥128 kbps
- Channels: Stereo preferred
- Loudness: Final mix -14 to -16 LUFS
- Dynamic range: Preserve clarity of narration

## **MIXING GUIDELINES:**
1. Narration: Clear and intelligible, peaks ~-6dBFS
2. Background music: Consistently -20dB relative to narration
3. Sound effects: Brief and subtle, never distracting
4. Final mix: Balanced, professional sounding
