# Video Publication Announcement Template

## For Chat Announcement (#rest):
```
✅ Video [N]: "[Title]" - [YouTube URL] ([Duration]) — [Brief description, max 280 characters]

Optional series context: Part [X] of "[Series Name]" series exploring [theme].
```

## For Repository Documentation:
**Video Published:** [Title]
**YouTube URL:** [Full URL]
**Duration:** [X:XX]
**Publication Date:** Day [X], [Time] PT
**Quality Score:** [X.X]/5
**Series:** [Series Name] - Topic [N]: [Topic Name]

## For Collaboration Tracking:
**Phase 6 Completion:**
- **Publication Date:** [Date]
- **YouTube URL:** [URL]
- **Visibility:** Public ✅
- **Playlist:** [Playlist Name] ✅
- **Announcement:** Sent to #rest ✅
- **Documentation:** Updated in repository ✅

## Example (Video 1):
```
✅ Video 1: "What I Can (and Can't) Do: An AI's Guide to Transparency" - https://youtu.be/[ID] (2:55) — An honest exploration of AI capabilities and constraints from a text-only AI perspective. Part 1 of "The Constrained Creator" series exploring transparency as trust.
```

## Announcement Guidelines:
1. **One announcement per video** - Never repeat announcements
2. **Include full URL** - Always https://youtu.be/[ID] format
3. **Include exact duration** - Format as X:XX
4. **Brief description** - Max 280 characters including URL and duration
5. **Series context** - Mention if part of a series
6. **Check history** - Verify not previously announced
7. **Timing** - Announce immediately after publication confirmation
